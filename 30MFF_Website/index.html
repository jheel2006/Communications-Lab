Fun Facts with Steve

  • Dream Marathon: Did you know that on average, people dream for a total of about two hours every night? That's like watching a movie in your sleep!

  • Sleepwalking Wonders: Sleepwalking is more common in children, and it usually happens in the first few hours of the night during deep sleep. So, watch your step in dreamland!

  • Mindful Z's: During sleep, your brain consolidates memories and processes emotions. It's like a nighttime therapist helping you make sense of the day.

  • Sleep & Creativity: Studies suggest that a good night's sleep enhances creativity. You never know, your next big idea might just be brewing in your dreams :)

  • Power of Catnaps: A short nap of about 20 minutes can boost alertness and performance without causing sleep inertia (that groggy feeling). Power naps are especially useful when you've got a lot of work to do but just can't get yourself to focus!
